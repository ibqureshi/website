About Me

Growing up as the youngest of four siblings, I was surrounded by a whirlwind of hobbies and passions that shaped my worldview. From snowboarding to capturing everyday moments through my camera, I’ve always found beauty in the world around me. Medicine captivated me with its intersection of human connection and scientific reasoning. It offers intellectual challenge, hands-on practice, and personal interaction—constantly pushing me to learn, adapt, and serve.

Being surrounded by family projects, our garage became a treasure trove of tools and materials. This upbringing drew me toward orthopedics, where I focus on making the broken functional again. Whether in the lab, clinic, or behind the camera, I’m excited to integrate science and art into a fulfilling medical career.

Research

Orthopedic Surgery and Sports Medicine

I’ve had the privilege of working on projects focusing on sports injuries and orthopedic surgery across various institutions. My work examines injury rates in professional sports leagues, with a focus on variables such as bye weeks, turf fields, and unique events like the Winter Olympics.

  • Injury Rates in the National Hockey League (NHL): Analysis of injury patterns before and after a midseason bye week.
  • Impact of the Winter Olympics on NHL Injuries: Investigating injury risks during Olympic years.
  • Turf vs. Grass in Major League Soccer (MLS): Assessing injury rates on different playing surfaces.

Unique Orthopedic Case Reports

Working with Dr. Mustafa Javed Bhalli, I have compiled case reports on unique orthopedic cases, providing valuable insights into unusual presentations and treatment approaches.

  • Orthopedic Injuries During Ramadan: A project assessing injury risks among Muslim footballers during Ramadan.

Female Authorship and Trends in Orthopedic Journals

I’ve been involved in evaluating female authorship trends in leading orthopedic journals, shedding light on the representation of female researchers.

  • Authorship Trends in Orthopedic Journals (2018-2023): Analyzing female first and senior author rates.

Geriatric Orthopedics and Multidisciplinary Care

I've contributed to reviews on geriatric patients, focusing on multidisciplinary care improving outcomes for older adults with rib fractures.

  • Rib Fracture Management in Older Adults: A scoping review exploring best practices for managing rib fractures.
